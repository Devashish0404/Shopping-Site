Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hotel and restaurant

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability and additional charges.

Do you offer parking facilities?

Yes, we provide complimentary parking for all our guests. The parking area is secure and monitored 24/7.

Is Wi-Fi available?

Yes, we offer free high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, including all rooms, restaurant, and common areas.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash payments.

Do you have a restaurant on-site?

Yes, we have an on-site restaurant serving authentic Indian cuisine. We offer breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Room service is also available.

Are pets allowed?

We have limited pet-friendly rooms available. Please inform us at the time of booking. Additional charges may apply.

Do you provide airport transfer services?

Yes, we offer airport transfer services at an additional charge. Please book this service in advance when making your reservation.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking free of charge up to 48 hours before check-in. For details, please visit our Cancellation Policy page.

Do you have facilities for events and meetings?

Yes, we have conference rooms and event spaces available. Please contact us for more information and bookings.

Is the hotel accessible for people with disabilities?

Yes, we have accessible rooms and facilities. Please see our Accessibility page for more details.
