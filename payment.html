Payment Options

Secure and convenient payment methods

Credit Cards

We accept all major credit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Debit Cards

All major debit cards are accepted for your convenience.

UPI

Pay using UPI through PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, and other UPI apps.

Net Banking

Direct bank transfer through net banking from all major banks.

Cash

Cash payments are accepted at the hotel reception.

Digital Wallets

Pay using digital wallets like Paytm, PhonePe, and Amazon Pay.

Security & Privacy

  • All transactions are secured with SSL encryption
  • We do not store your complete card details
  • Payment information is processed through secure payment gateways
  • Your financial data is protected and never shared with third parties

Payment Terms

Advance Payment

For bookings made more than 7 days in advance, a 30% advance payment is required to confirm your reservation.

Full Payment

Full payment is required at check-in. For online bookings, you can choose to pay in full or pay the advance amount.

Refund Policy

Refunds are processed according to our cancellation policy. Please refer to our Cancellation Policy for details.
