Cancellation Policy

Understand our cancellation and refund policies

Cancellation Terms

Free Cancellation

Cancel your booking up to 48 hours before check-in for a full refund (minus processing fees).

48-24 Hours Before Check-in

50% refund of the total booking amount (minus processing fees).

Less than 24 Hours

No refund available. However, we may offer credit for future stays at our discretion.

Refund Process

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method
  • Processing fees (if applicable) are non-refundable
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed

How to Cancel

Online

Use our Track Booking page to cancel your reservation online.

Phone

Call us at +91 XXXXX XXXXX during business hours (9 AM - 9 PM IST).

Email

Send your cancellation request to info@captainhotel.com with your booking reference number.

Special Cases

No-Show

If you don't show up for your reservation without prior cancellation, the full amount will be charged.

Force Majeure

In case of natural disasters, pandemics, or government restrictions, we offer flexible rebooking or full refunds.
