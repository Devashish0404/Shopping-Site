Accessibility

Committed to providing accessible services for all guests

Our Commitment

Captain Hotel & Restaurant is committed to ensuring that our facilities and services are accessible to all guests, including those with disabilities. We strive to comply with accessibility standards and continuously work to improve our services.

Accessible Facilities

Accessible Rooms

We offer accessible rooms with features including:

  • Wider doorways and accessible bathrooms
  • Grab bars in bathrooms
  • Lowered light switches and thermostats
  • Roll-in showers or accessible bathtubs
  • Visual and audible fire alarms

Public Areas

  • Ramp access to main entrance
  • Accessible parking spaces
  • Elevator access to all floors
  • Accessible restaurant and dining areas
  • Wheelchair-accessible restrooms

Website Accessibility

We are committed to making our website accessible to all users. Our website includes:

  • Alt text for images
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast options
  • Resizable text

Service Animals

Service animals are welcome at Captain Hotel & Restaurant. We comply with all applicable laws regarding service animals and do not charge additional fees for service animals.

Special Assistance

If you require special assistance during your stay, please contact us in advance:

  • We can arrange for sign language interpreters (with advance notice)
  • We provide written materials in accessible formats upon request
  • Our staff is trained to assist guests with disabilities
  • We can accommodate special dietary requirements
